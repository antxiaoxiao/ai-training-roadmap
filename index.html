Phase 1 · Month 1–3
Build internal trust, refine content
The one most important thing this phase
Take the DeLaval project workflow — auditing the design system with Claude + Figma MCP — and document it with screenshots and time-saved data. This is the first cornerstone of your entire brand.
Phase 2 · Month 4–6
Go public, build visibility
The one most important thing this phase
Publish your first bilingual LinkedIn post: "How I used AI to cut a 2-hour design system audit down to 10 minutes." Use real numbers — that's what makes it shareable.
Phase 3 · Month 7–9
Deliver externally, validate market
The one most important thing this phase
Run your first external workshop — even if it's free. Actually delivering once teaches you more than three more months of preparation. Real feedback is the asset.
Phase 4 · Month 10–12
Systematise, build brand assets
The one most important thing this phase
Record your most popular training module as a video course and publish it on Gumroad or Maven. This is the shift from trading time for money to content generating income.
